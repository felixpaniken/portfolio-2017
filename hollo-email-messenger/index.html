2016 / Product Lead Hollo - email was boring

The problem

A lot of people use email every day, but it's not a pleasant experience.

Solution

The structure of email made it hard to use. Who did I talk to about what? What was the subject? The solution was to change the hierarchy of email, who are you communicating with? That is what's most important.

So yeah.. that's like any messaging client out there. But let's keep what is good about email front and center. Subjects, attachments, group conversations. This needs to just work and be amazing.

Easily switch between subjects, or make a new one!

There's also all those other emails, let's keep the separate from the conversations with real humans.
