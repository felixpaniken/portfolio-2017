2018 / Project Lead Discovery - The future of app stores

The problem

While working at Aptoide, we found that users don't engage and discovery with the store.

Solution

Since app discovery happens outside of the App Store, let's not build "another app store"

When looking at the behaviour of our users and doing user interviews we discovered that app discovery usually happened outside of the app store. Sure, sometimes users would go to the app store and browse around, maybe via categories and the like but a lot of users mentioned hearing about an app trough other sources going to the app store only to get that one app.

This finding is not neccesarily a problem, but it is a great opportunity! My Manager at the time tasked me and one developer with coming up with "the future of app stores".

Our explorations went different ways but in the end we wanted to capture two things learnt from our current users in Aptoide. Our users do not install new Apps that often when compared to Games. This makes sense since after playing a game, you might want another one or more of the same, either case you will be on the hunt. Compare this to how often users install and looks for new social media platforms.

And the result? TikTok meets twitch for mobile games only! Discovery is a platform where any user can easily record themself playing a mobile game and upload it to their channel to share it with others. It's just as easy to watch other peoples gaming moments, as a user you get content immediately and swipt to the right to see the next video.

Have a click around!

The way a user can record their gaming moment is easy, an overlay from Discovery sits on top of whatever game the user is playing. One tap and the recording starts. The user could then further customize their video with gifs before uploading to Discovery.
