2018 / Project Lead Discovery - The future of app stores

The problem

While working at Aptoide (a third party android app store), we found that users don't engage as much as we wanted with our app store. We need an app store that users visits multiple times per day!

Solution

Since app discovery happens outside of the App Store, let's not build "another app store".

When looking at the behavior of our users and doing user interviews we discovered that app discovery usually happened outside of the app store. A common pattern was hearing about an app from a friend, seeing it on youtube, etc.

Our explorations went different ways but in the end we wanted to capture two things learnt from our current users in Aptoide:

  • Our users do not install new Apps often BUT they do install a lot of games. This makes sense since after playing a game, you might want another one or more of the same, either case you will be on the hunt.
  • Our users love videos and videos were always some of the most engaged with content.
